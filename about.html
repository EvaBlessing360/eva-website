A bit about me

I'm Blessing. Most people call me Eva.

Five years in school for this. Two years on the ground. And one promise — I won't bring you anything I haven't put my own money behind.

Hi, I'm Blessing.

Most people call me Eva. Some call me The Real Estate Queen. I answer to both — but at heart, I'm a connector.

I formally studied real estate for five years before I sold my first plot. While many of my peers chased "quick deals," I took the long way around — learning the legal frameworks, the title structures, the surveying realities, and the diaspora pain points that most agents either ignore or never understand.

For the last two years I've put that training to work as a full-time practitioner — selling 25+ plots of land, closing on 5+ houses, and booking 50+ short-let stays for clients in Lagos, Ibadan, and Ogun State. Many of them are Nigerians abroad who needed someone they could trust on the ground. Some are first-time local buyers. A few are travelers who just needed a clean short-let when they fly home.

What they share is simple: they all wanted someone who would tell them the truth. That's what I do. I connect you to the right opportunity at the right time — with every document, title, and verification in order — and I tell you honestly when something isn't worth your money.

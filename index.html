Meet Eva

Not just an agent. A connector.

I'm Blessing Collins — most people call me Eva. I studied real estate for five years and have been actively serving clients for two. In that short time, I've moved over 15 plots and several hectares of land for buyers across Lagos, Ibadan, and beyond.

My belief is simple: real estate isn't just transactions — it's timing, trust, and the right relationship. Some clients are buying their family home. Others are quietly building wealth from abroad. Some just want a clean short-let when they fly in. My job is to connect you to the opportunity that fits your season.

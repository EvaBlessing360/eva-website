A bit about me

Hi, I'm Blessing.

Most people call me Eva. I studied real estate for five years before I sold my first plot — and I've been doing this full time for two. In that time: 25+ plots sold, 5+ houses closed, 50+ short-lets booked. Most of those clients are still in my phone. Some have already come back for the second deal.

Here's the part that matters. I don't sell anything I haven't checked myself. I've walked the boundaries. I've read the survey. I've sat in the lawyer's office. And if a deal isn't clean, I'll be the first one to tell you to wait — even if it costs me the commission.

That's the whole job. Tell you the truth. Show you something worth your money. Stay in the conversation long after the keys change hands.

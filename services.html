Service 01

Land Sales — Lagos & Ibadan

Land is the foundation of Nigerian wealth — but it's also where the most expensive mistakes are made. I only connect you to land with clean, verified titles.

What's included:

  • Title verification: C of O, Governor's Consent, Deed of Assignment, Excision & Gazette — I know which applies and how to confirm it.
  • Location due diligence: Flood checks, access roads, neighborhood growth trajectory, government plans (BRT, rail, road projects).
  • Structured payment plans: For diaspora and long-term buyers, many of my estates offer 6-24 month installment options.
  • Plots and hectares: Whether you want one plot for your future home or several hectares for development, I source both.

Typical client: Young professionals buying their first plot, diaspora investors, family acquisitions, developers acquiring for resale or build-and-hold.

Service 03

Farm Investments — Alabata, Ogun State

Most "farm investments" you've heard about are crypto-style schemes — you give money, you hope. The opportunities I market are different. You buy real land, you own it 100%, and you earn revenue every harvest cycle from short-cycle crops the Nigerian market cannot get enough of.

How it works:

  • Buy a farm plot in Alabata, Odede LGA, Ogun State — full ownership, registered in your name.
  • Pay a one-time farm setup fee — covers land prep, planting, fencing, drip irrigation, and first-cycle inputs.
  • Earn revenue every harvest cycle — short-cycle crops mean multiple harvests per year, not multi-year waits.
  • The developer manages everything — planting, labor, harvest, and sales. You receive periodic updates and your share of revenue.
  • The land is yours forever. Even after harvests, the asset keeps appreciating.

What's grown there:

🌶️ Pepper · 🍅 Tomato · 🥒 Cucumber · 🍠 Sweet Potato

These are the crops Nigeria's domestic market consumes daily — short growing cycles, predictable demand, and prices that have only gone up.

Marketed by Terrax Properties Limited · Developed by Paradise Farms by Agroscale.
I represent this opportunity to my clients — every plot is verified, every setup fee is documented, and I stay involved through the harvest cycle.

Typical client: Diaspora investors who want tangible Nigerian assets, professionals who want passive income on top of land ownership, ethical portfolio builders who like productive land.
